Property Management Services In South East Of London

At Key Regency, we offer a complete property management service designed to make your life easier. From regular property inspections and tenant sourcing to handling paperwork, rent collection, and day-to-day communication, we take care of it all with professionalism and attention to detail.

We also provide full maintenance support to keep your property in top condition, including access to a reliable 24-hour callout service when urgent issues arise. Whether you own one property or several, you can count on us to manage every part of the process with care and efficiency.

We provide expert property management services across South East and Greater London, helping landlords and tenants enjoy a hassle-free experience. Whether you're in the heart of the city or in one of the surrounding boroughs, our team is on hand to support you with local knowledge and responsive service. Below is a list of the key areas we cover — if you don't see your location listed but are nearby, feel free to get in touch. We're always happy to help.

Commercial & Domestic Properties

We manage properties of all types, whether you own residential flats or commercial buildings.

Construction & Renovation

From full property rebuilds to loft conversions and extensions — we bring your ideas to life with quality workmanship.

Tenant Management

End-to-end support including sourcing reliable tenants, handling rent collection, and managing evictions with legal care.

Quick, reliable repair services and general upkeep to keep your property safe and functional.

Gardening Services
